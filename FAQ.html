Frequently Asked Questions

Need Blood Immediately. Which app finds patients blood requirement as soon as possible?
At Google Play Store, you can search Blood Finder. Once installed, using GPS technology the app locates the nearest blood bank along with blood availability.
I had alcohol before going to donate blood. Is it okay?
No. We do not take blood from anyone under the influence of alcohol. Being intoxicated can affect your ability to understand and answer the donor questionnaire and declaration.
I am taking antibiotics. Can I donate blood?
It depends on why you are taking the antibiotics and may also depend on doctor's advice.
Are there any side effects of blood donation?
There are no significant side effects. Blood bank staff ensures that your donation experience is safe and comfortable. Some donors have donated 25-100 times in their lifetime.
How often can I donate blood?
You can donate blood after every three to four months.
What should I eat before blood donation?
Eat your usual home meals. A light snack and a soft drink before donation are sufficient.
Where can I find latest blood stock from various blood banks?
You can check blood stock in the Stock Availability page of the app.
Where can I find details of camps being conducted by various blood banks?
Camp details are available in the Camp Schedule page. Many camps also allow online pre-registration.
How can I onboard my blood bank to Blood Finder?
You can register your blood bank using the Add Blood Center form available in the app.
Whom should I contact in case of any problem?
Kindly email your queries to support@bloodfinder.com.
How to check platelet units in the app?
Search for stock in the Stock Availability page, select "Platelet" as component type, and search again.
How can I check the nearest blood bank from my location?
Enable location in the app and search for the nearest blood bank. You will see a list sorted by distance.
How to verify if blood unit is available on the current date?
Check the "Last Updated" or "LIVE" status in the Blood Availability option.
How does age affect my ability to donate blood?
Minimum age for whole blood donation is 18 years. Maximum age depends on donation type.
Eligibility questions before donating blood
  • Are you currently taking any medication (e.g., antibiotics, blood thinners)? No
  • Are you between 18 to 65 years of age? Yes
  • Is your body weight at least 45 kg? Yes
  • Have you had any infection, fever, cold, cough, weakness, dizziness, or fatigue today? No
  • Have you undergone any surgery or major dental procedure recently (last 6–12 months)? No
  • Did you have at least 6 hours of sleep last night? Yes
  • Did you eat a light (non-oily) meal 2–3 hours before donating? Yes
  • Have you had any tattoos, piercings, or acupuncture in the last 6 months? No
  • Have you consumed alcohol in the last 24 hours? No
