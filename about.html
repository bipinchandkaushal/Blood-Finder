🌟 About Blood Finder

Our Mission

Blood Finder is dedicated to saving lives by connecting people in need of blood with nearby hospitals and donors quickly and efficiently. Our goal is to make blood donation and availability accessible, transparent, and reliable for everyone.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no one dies waiting for blood. By leveraging technology and building a strong network of hospitals and donors, Blood Finder aims to streamline blood availability and emergency support across India.

How It Helps

  • Search for available blood groups in nearby hospitals
  • Request blood for emergencies directly from hospitals
  • Get guidance and tips on blood donation
  • Access donation history and notifications (for registered hospitals)
